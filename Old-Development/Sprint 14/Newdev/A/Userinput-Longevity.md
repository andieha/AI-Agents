## User Preferences – Longevity

### Include

**Research & Science**
- Sleep: sleep quality, sleep stages, circadian rhythm, sleep duration, sleep debt
- Nutrition: longevity diets (Mediterranean, Blue Zone), caloric restriction, protein intake, micronutrients, anti-inflammatory foods
- Exercise: VO2 max, zone 2 cardio, strength training, HIIT, mobility, muscle mass and longevity
- Fasting: intermittent fasting, time-restricted eating, prolonged fasting, autophagy
- Supplements: NMN, NAD+, resveratrol, metformin, rapamycin, omega-3, vitamin D, magnesium, creatine
- Cognitive health: neuroplasticity, dementia prevention, brain training, memory, Alzheimer's risk reduction
- Stress and recovery: cortisol, HRV (heart rate variability), meditation, breathwork, cold exposure, sauna
- Biomarkers and testing: blood panels, VO2 max testing, DEXA scans, continuous glucose monitoring, biological age clocks
- Gut health: microbiome, probiotics, prebiotics, gut-brain axis
- Longevity research: clinical trials, hallmarks of aging, senescence, epigenetics, telomeres

**People & Channels to Follow**
- Peter Attia (lifespan, healthspan, performance medicine)
- Rhonda Patrick (nutrition, supplements, heat/cold stress)
- Andrew Huberman (neuroscience, protocols, circadian biology)
- Bryan Johnson (Blueprint protocol, biological age reversal)
- David Sinclair (NAD+, epigenetic reprogramming, aging biology)
- Mark Hyman (functional medicine, nutrition)

### Avoid

- Unverified supplements and miracle-cure claims without peer-reviewed backing
- Clickbait and fearmongering health content
- Influencers with no scientific credentials or field experience
- Anti-aging pseudoscience and detox culture
- Armchair health commentators with no clinical or research background
